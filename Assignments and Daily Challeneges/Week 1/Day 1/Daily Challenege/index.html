CAPTAIN AMERICA

As a recipient of the Super-Solder serum, WWII hero Steve Rogers fights for American ideals as one of the worlds mightiest heros and the leader of the Avengers. With his powerful shield and an unwaivering code of honor, Captain America fights to protect the United States and all of mankind from its greatest enemines.

Learn more about Captain America at the official Capital America Website or at Wikipedia.
